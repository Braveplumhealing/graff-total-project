Meet the Guide

About Johnny

Life coach, Reiki master, author, podcast host, retreat facilitator, and mediator — in service of your fullest life.

Johnny is a transformational life coach, Reiki master, author, mediator, and retreat facilitator dedicated to helping people reconnect with their deepest wisdom and step boldly into the life they are meant to live.

With a warm, grounded presence and a genuine gift for meeting people exactly where they are, Johnny blends the precision of evidence-based coaching with the quiet power of energy healing. His work has guided hundreds of individuals through pivotal life transitions.

His Story

Johnny’s path to this work was shaped by his own seasons of change. After years of professional success that left him feeling hollow, he discovered that true fulfilment lives in alignment — when the life you are building on the outside matches the truth you carry on the inside. That discovery changed everything. Today he brings that hard-won clarity to every session, workshop, and retreat — with warmth, presence, and deep care for each person.
