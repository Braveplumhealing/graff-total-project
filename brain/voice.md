# Johnny's voice & sacred language

The durable home for **how Johnny sounds** and the core concepts that carry his work — so
every talk, page, and word we write for him rings true. Feeds the four keynotes, the site,
and everything else. Read this before writing anything in Johnny's voice.

**Iron rule:** these are *Johnny's own* concepts. We NEVER invent or "fix" their meaning.
His definitions come from him, or from his book, *Soulnapped: The Eagle in the Coop*. Where
we don't yet have his exact words, we mark `[Johnny to define]` and ask — we never guess his
sacred language on a page or a stage.

## The sacred triad (Johnny named these, 2026-07-13)
Johnny asked us to center three components. They read as a natural arc — quite possibly
**THE spine of his work** — but the meanings below are only a gentle first reading to confirm
*with him*, never asserted as his:

1. **Sacred exhaustion** — `[Johnny to define]`
   *(tentative read, to confirm: the holy tiredness of carrying a life that isn't yours —
   burnout not as failure but as a sacred signal that something true is asking to be lived.)*
2. **Sacred interruption** — `[Johnny to define]`
   *(tentative: the moment that stops you — grace breaking in, the turn, the thing that won't
   let you keep performing.)*
3. **Rising mercy** — `[Johnny to define]`
   *(tentative: the compassion that lifts you afterward — mercy toward yourself, coming home,
   the gentle grace that raises the eagle out of the coop.)*

## How to use them
- Candidate spine for **all four talks**, beginning with *The Aligned Life*:
  **sacred exhaustion → sacred interruption → rising mercy.**
- This is Johnny's *own* sacred language and almost certainly belongs at the center, in place
  of (or deepening) the working "Notice · Name · Nudge" frame — **once his meanings are
  confirmed.** Do not weave the triad into any public/spoken piece until Johnny defines each
  term (or we have it from *Soulnapped*).
- When his definitions land: re-run the talk design so the arc, the cold open, and the closing
  charge all rise through these three movements. Link back to [[the-aligned-life]].
