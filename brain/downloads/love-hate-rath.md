## Offered unpackings (for Johnny to keep, prune, or reject)
1. **Wrath is not hate alone.** Nobody rages over what they don't care about. Hate alone
   is cold. Add love — betrayed, wounded, entangled — and you get fire. Wrath = love's
   energy driven through hate's channel.
2. **They don't cancel — which quietly proves hate is not love's opposite.** If they were
   true opposites the sum would be zero (indifference). The equation says the sum is
   SOMETHING — so hate must be made of the same caring current, reversed.
3. **The mercy inside the equation:** because love remains a component of wrath, wrath is
   RECOVERABLE — un-braid it and the love is still there. The wrathful person is closer to
   love than the numb one. (Rhymes with rising mercy: even the truck was love.)
4. **The fortress reading (the accident worth keeping):** a *rath* is an ancient Irish
   ringfort — a circular earthen fortress. Love braided with hate builds a rath around the
   heart: armor, the bolted door, the defended pane.
5. **Where it may land:** the mediation table — wrath in the room is PROOF of love in the
   room ("they're fighting to be heard"). Possibly circling the empty center seat of
   Conflict as Catalyst — noted, not forced.
