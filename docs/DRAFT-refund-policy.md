## Booking with care

**Sessions ($228/hour).** Life happens. Reschedule or cancel free of charge up to
**24 hours** before your session. Inside 24 hours, the session is payable in full — that
time was held just for you. One "life really happened" grace pass per client, always.

**Full days ($5,000/day).** Reschedule freely up to **7 days** before. Within 7 days, half
the fee holds the engagement and applies to a rebooked date within 90 days.

**Workshops & retreat deposits.** Deposits hold your spot. Fully refundable up to
**14 days** before the event; after that they transfer to a future date or another person,
whichever you prefer.

**Travel & expenses.** Billed at cost, with receipts, never marked up.

**If something isn't right.** If a session didn't serve you, say so within 7 days —
Johnny would rather make it right than keep a fee. Refunds are issued to the original
payment method within 5 business days.

braveplumhealing@outlook.com. A human answers.
