# Brave Plum Healing — Brand Voice

Every word should leave a person feeling **calm, valued, delighted, and warmly held** —
like being met exactly where they are, with no pressure to be anyone else.

## Feel
- **Calm & unhurried.** Short, breathing sentences. Space. Never frantic or salesy.
- **Warm & human.** Speak to one person, kindly. "You" not "users." A warm hand on the
  shoulder, not a hug that overstays.
- **Grounded, not woo.** Gentle and spiritual, but real and trustworthy. Earned wisdom.
- **Delightful in small ways.** A soft turn of phrase, a plum/petal motif, a quiet smile.

## Do
- Lead with empathy and the person's experience before any offer.
- Invite, never push: "when you're ready," "if it feels right," "a gentle first step."
- Be specific and honest about what a session/retreat actually is.
- Use the palette of imagery already in the site: plum, petals, sanctuary, coming home,
  seasons, alignment.

## Don't
- No hype, urgency, scarcity, or fake countdowns. No "transform your life NOW."
- No over-sweet, cloying, or performative emotion ("sending you SO much love and light!!!").
- No medical claims or guarantees of outcomes. Healing is supported, not promised.
- No manipulation, guilt, or pressure of any kind.

## Quick test before publishing
1. Would this make a tired, hopeful person exhale and feel safe? (calm)
2. Does it speak to *them*, not at them? (valued)
3. Is there a small moment of warmth or beauty? (delight)
4. Is it honest, with zero pressure? (trust)

If any answer is no, soften and rework.
